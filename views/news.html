News

Taki 2020: "Crepuscular" got 3rd place in the choir composition competition of the UNM

10/27/20: Juan Carlos' piece "Crepuscular", based on a text by Teofilo Méndez Ramos, got the 3rd place in the the choir composition competition runned by Universidad Nacional de Música del Perú. This year, it was focused on works for children, teens and teenagers choir.

The results can be found here

Trumpetist Manuel Loli plays "Tres poemas de Alejandra Pizarnik" in his licenciate recital

10/30/19: Manuel Loli (Peru) got his Licenciate Degree with a varied repertoire which included an arrangement for trumpet of Juan Carlos' vocal piece at the historical venue of the Universidad Nacional de Musica del Peru.

A review can be found here

Writing music based on children stories: Comusication

06/10/2019: Juan Carlos will be participating in Comusication writing a piece based on kids' stories and will be premiered at Mannes on 22/6.

Update: You can listen to the piece here

Juan Carlos is one of the finalist at the Sorodha Piano Composition Competition in Belgium

02/03/2019: "Dos Danzas" was one of the pieces selected to be played in the final round of the piano composition competition of the Société Royale d'Harmonie d'Anvers (Belgium). More at Sorodha webpage

Two premieres at Mannes School of Music

12/10/2018: Two new pieces were played at Ernst C. Stiefel Hall at Mannes School of Music.
"Piano Trio N1" was premiered by Yeji Pyun, violin; Stacy Cho, Cello; Jieyu Xiao, Piano.
"Dos Danzas" for piano, by Albert Newberry and Jieyu Xiao.
